Bridge Output Program

Associated Application

An auxiliary program (output.exe) that calculates bridge outputs for six different Wheatstone bridge arrangements.

Configurations

Select from the six bridge configurations using the RADIO BUTTON:

1 + v Arms, Axial/Bending Half-bridge circuit typically used in low cost transducers. Nonlinear.

2 Arms, Bending/Shear Half-bridge circuit for low cost transducers as above except higher output and linear behavior.

2 + 2v Arms, Axial/Bending Full-bridge circuit with two gages aligned with maximum principal strain and two Poisson gages. Typically used on column transducers. Nonlinear.

2 + 2v Arms, Bending Full-bridge circuit with two gages aligned with maximum principal strain and two Poisson gages. Typically used on bending beams. Linear.

4 Arms, Differential Full-bridge circuit used on bending beams to cancel bending moments. All four gages are aligned with the maximum principal strain but two gages are mounted in area of ï¿½higherï¿½ strain than the other two. Bridge output is proportional to the difference in strain at the two gage locations irrespective of their actual strain magnitudes. Nonlinear.

4 Arms, Bending/Shear Full-bridge circuit with four fully active gages. Two gages aligned with maximum principal tension strain and two gages aligned with maximum principal compression strain. Linear.
