C-Pattern Resistors

Circuits Refinements command

C-Pattern resistors are used when a bonded foil resistor that can be adjusted is need in Balco alloy . These are normally used to determine the correct value of resistance before ordering an A-Pattern resistor of fixed value.

C-Pattern resistors are available in four configurations:

C11 Configuration 5 ohms (uncut) to 12 ohms (cut)

C12-10 Configuration 10 ohms (uncut) to 24 ohms (cut)

C12-20 Configuration 20 ohms (uncut) to 48 ohms (cut)

C13 Configuration 40 or 80 ohms (uncut) to 96 or 192 ohms (cut) ohms
